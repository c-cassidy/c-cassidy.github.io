# c-cassidy.github.io Project: Index

Sightseeing with a snail









The Torridon

Location: Scotland
If you ever need time to yourself, this is the hotel to come to. Less than a 5 minute walk from Loch Torridon, this hotel is known for their impressive array of outdoor activities. After enjoying nature, one can enjoy their delicious restaurants.

Clontarf Castle

Location: Ireland
This former castle is a perfect mix of new and old. Its old character is accented with a lot of modern touches. If you want to leave the castle, it is easy to explore nearby Dublin for a day.

The Sebel Quay West

With direct views of the Sydney Opera House, this hotel is a perfect place to stay while in Sydney. The most amazing part of this hotel is their beautiful pool. This pool has some of the best views in the city.

Hotel Majestic

This high tier hotel exemplifies the beautiful architecture one associates with Italy. After waking up, don’t forget to enjoy their rooftop cafe with the sounds of the city in the background.

Remota

Location: Chile
As its name would suggest, this hotel is truly remote. Situated in the Chilean Patagonia, staying at this hotel makes one feel at home in nature.

Hayman Island Resort

Location: Australia
Sitting in a beautiful island in the Whitsundays, this resort is the ultimate beach vacation. Enjoy the beautiful scenery while basking in the sun on the beach.

Cradle Mountain Lodge

Location: Australia
Another isolated location, this camp style lodge is situated at the base of cradle mountain. Oftentimes, one can wake up and see a kangaroo or a wallaby outside their room!

Ahwahnee Hotel Yosemite

Location: Yosemite National Park
If you ever want to experience one of America’s best national parks firsthand, this is the hotel to come to. Immerse yourself with nature while enjoying a fun hike.

Hotel Cumbres Lastarria

Location: Chile
This modern hotel is the place to stay if you visit Santiago. Their fun rooms are a great place to return to after a day of exploring the city.

Ritz Carlton Kapalua

Location: Hawaii
Who doesn’t want to go to Hawaii? While there, make sure to relax in this beautiful, seaside hotel.

Kimpton Seafire Cayman

Location: Cayman Islands
Known for their quirky hotels, Kimpton established themselves in the Cayman Islands with this Seafire Resort. It is cutely decorated and designed to help you relax at the beach.

Shinta Mani Shack

Location: Cambodia
If you ever are in Siem Reap, this is the hotel to be in. It is very relaxing and the staff constantly has aromatic candles / scents in the hallway. This is a great hotel to return after exploring nearby Angkor Wat.

La Sante

Location: Vietnam
This hotel has amazing staff and is situated in a lively part of Hanoi. Staying here might even help you find some of the locals’ best hangouts.

Carmel Valley Ranch

Location: California
Enjoy a nice spa day or a round of golf at this beautiful hotel.

Hotel Del Coronado

Location: California
This distinct hotel is famous for its beachside experience.

Arizona Biltmore

Location: Arizona
Enjoy all that Arizona has to offer with this beautiful hotel. One could even take a day trip to the Grand Canyon from this great accommodation.
